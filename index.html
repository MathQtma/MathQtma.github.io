Research Interests

Qianting MA is Associate Professor of Applied Mathematics at the School of Mathematics and Statistics, Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology. She obtained the Ph.D. degree in applied mathematics from Zhejiang University, China, in 2017, and worked as a postdoc at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, from 2020 to 2022. She is a member of China Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (CSIAM), member of CSIAM Activity Group on Mathematics and Medicine Interdisciplinary, and a member of Chinese Society of Biomedical Engineering (CSBME) Activity Group on Artificial Intelligence in Medicine. She also served as a reviewer for many journals, including IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology, Scientific Reports, Journal of Visual Communication and Image Representation and Computers in Biology and Medicine. Her research interests include variational methods and deep learning methods for image processing, inverse problems in imaging, optimization techniques and applications in imaging, as well as nonlinear analysis and scientific computing. Her research has been supported by National Natural Science Foundation of China and The High-Level Innovative and Entrepreneurial Project in Jiangsu Province, China.
